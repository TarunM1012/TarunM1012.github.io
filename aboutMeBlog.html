About Me

I'm Tarun Modekurty, a car and computer enthusiast. The best of the combination leads to my interest in Sim Racing where I race the ARCA Menards Series, NASCAR Truck Series, Fanatec Street Stock Series, MX-5 Series, and occasionally the iRacing Rallycross Series. In my time that I'm not racing, I enjoy playing the guitar, and learn more about computers and cybersecurity for which I compete in many different competitions. I am a 3 time State Cybersecurity finalist as well.
